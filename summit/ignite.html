Submit a Talk – Ignite CfA!

Submit your idea now!

The evening of Wednesday, September 24, join us for snacks, drinks, and fun at Ignite #CfASummit at the Code for America Summit.

An Ignite is a story that's exactly 5 minutes long, told by a speaker who's working with 20 slides that auto-advance every 15 seconds (ready or not). It's fun, it's fast, and the format forces you to really think about what you want to say.

So what do you want to talk about?

We — and 200 of your closest Summit friends — want to hear your cool ideas, hacks, lessons learned and "war stories." Your talk can be geeky, fanciful, poignant, educational, and/or funny. Submit your ideas today!

DEADLINE TO SUBMIT: Wednesday, September 17, 11:59 p.m. PDT. Accepted speakers will be notified by Friday, September 19, 3:00 p.m. PDT.

If you haven't seen an Ignite talk, perhaps you'd like to:

  • Watch some videos at igniteshow.com
  • Watch Scott Berkun's "Why and How to Give an Ignite Talk"
  • Read Jason Grigsby's "How to Give a Successful Ignite Presentation"
  • And submit a talk!
