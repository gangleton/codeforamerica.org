Mayors are Coding for America

Mayors from cities around the country are building innovation capabilities to tackle the challenges of today and tomorrow.

Make sure your city is at the table. Nominate a representative of your administration to be part of a nationwide cohort of cities and counties who are reimagining innovation for the 21st century.

Lead the Way & Join the Conversation

Ready to dive in? Nominate your most forward-thinking staff to join a select group of governments in the Code for America Peer Network. As part of the group, your staff will:

  • Learn about best practices and cutting edge technology from around the country, and connect directly with experts who have first-hand experience.
  • Get individualized support from Code for America staff and advisors to build innovation capabilities suited to your city's unique needs, strengths, and priorities.
  • Have the opportunity to attend the Code for America Summit and participate in hands-on training to build 21st century innovation capabilities.
