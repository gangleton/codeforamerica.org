Greg Kerwin

Member of

  • Staff

Greg Kerwin (Chief Revenue & Marketing Officer) brings 20 years of business development, marketing and product management experience to Code for America. He comes from UBM where he developed leading global events and digital media products, including collaborating with O’Reilly Media on the successful Web 2.0 and Gov 2.0 events and GTEC, Canada’s leading government technology event. He has managed the launch and acquisition of numerous tech media products, and has extensive global experience in more than 15 countries including India, China, Brazil, Japan, Indonesia, Turkey, Russia and throughout Western Europe. Greg began his career in the non-profit sector and worked on a number of political campaigns. He holds a degree in Economics from the University of Michigan and recently moved back to Oakland, CA with his wife and three children after a two-year stint in the Netherlands.
